TV and Movie Reviews

This is a funny one is a lot of ways, at least in regards to me. These days, I do not really watch TV shows or movies, not because I dislike them, but because there are things I prefer, like how recently I have started to more actively read books. I will still watch a movie here and now, and there are certain shows that when they get a new season, I just have to watch it, but I have trouble starting new shows, I donâ€™t really know why.

I am a little more active when it comes to watching movies, especially new ones, like Oppenheimer for example, or the next Dune movie. I find this odd for myself, because I have always preferred longer stories, a full, long story arc, even when it comes to movies. My favourite movies are the Lord of the Rings movies, and I have love for many other film franchises like Harry Potter, James Bond, and more. I do also love very many standalone movies, my favourite being 12 Angry Men, which will be reviewed below.

I have the feeling that my motivation for TV series, especially live-action one has died down following the ending of Game of Thrones. That show had me hooked, and I still obsess over the books to this day, but that ending not only disappointed me, but only tired me out, the constant discussion on social-media about how bad it was, and how the books would never finish, just made my frustration with the show expand. The reviews made here will vary in length and will more than likely be brief. I plan to make longer form reviews or analyses of these stories, most likely to be done in the form of a YouTube video.
