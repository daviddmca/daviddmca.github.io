Sports

The other of my two loves or hobbies is sports. I have been watching and playing sports for half a decade now, and while I have my favourites, I pretty much enjoy any sport. My favourites are Hockey, Football (for my fellow North Americans, this is Soccer), Basketball, swimming and running. My love for thes come from the athletes, teams, and the moments/events.

One of the avenues of writing that I have wanted to get involved in is sports journalism, and it is still an industry that I have an interest in working in. For a start, I thought focusing on the athletes, teams, and moments that made me fall in love with sport wsa the right place for me to start.
