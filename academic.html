Academic Writing

As I am a current student, majority of the writing that I have done the past few years, and most of my life really, has been academic. The range of what I have to write about and the style and format I write is different. There are samples of writing below, with a brief description for the writing pieces, for example the context behind the assignment, why I went about writing it the way I did. Note that the writing pieces can range in quality based on when I wrote it, or how much time was put into it.

While I do call it writing, it is not exclusively prose writing. There may be a video, or writing that includes images, etc.

Creative Writing Project: In this project I wrote stories with a corressponding illustration. This was very much an ameteur writing attempt, and I have problems with the writing, but I am very proud of the concept. While this was an asignment for class, it could also be seen as simply fiction writing.
