Sports Moments

The other of my two loves or hobbies is sports. I have been watching and playing sports for half a decade now, and while I have my favourites, I pretty much enjoy any sport. My favourites are Hockey, Football (for my fellow North Americans, this is Soccer), Basketball, swimming and running. Part of the reason that these ones are my favourite is because of what made me fall in love with these sports are specific moments, and that is the high I chase every time I watch sports, yes, the sport itself is entertaining, but those certain moments that almost seem life-changing, and at some points are, are so powerful.

I loved and still love sports so much, that I wanted, and in many ways still want to be a sports journalist. To give a specific moment that made me love sports so strongly, I would have to go back to the 2010 Winter Olympics in Vancouver, this being the first time I had watched the Olympics, or at least remember watching the Olympics. On the final day of the games, Sidney Crosby scored the “Golden Goal” which gave Canada victory over the USA, in the men’s ice hockey final. I don’t remember being more jubilant over something in my life.

Since then I have had many other moments that made me fall in love with sports, and also frustrated at the same time. While I do wish to focus on the ones that I enjoyed, I will more than likely include the ones that I did not enjoy, like when a team I loved lost for example. I do hope to one day make this a series in the form of YouTube videos, but on this page, I simply want to have it written down.
