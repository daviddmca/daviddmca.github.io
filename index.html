As a writer I have a great love and want for writing about fiction, from writing my own stories and the many works in progresses that is, as well as writing reviews of pieces of fiction, especially the ones that mean something to me. I also want to write deep dives into what makes certain pieces of fiction a favourite of mine, and how it shaped me as a person and my personal taste in stories.

Along with fiction, another great love of mine is sports, and I would like to incorporate that into my writing in some kind of way. This could include things like speaking about why I like certain sports, or athletes, or certain moments in sports history that I personally love and why sports are something I love.

I have written reviews for websites, but they are very brief and lack the depth I would like them to have. If I could do reviews, I would actually like to try them in the video format, which I would add to the website as they are made.

When it comes to what I value in my writing and others writing is an emotional connection to the writing. You should care in some kind of way about what you are writing about, whether it is about a hobby, yourself, or any other kind of things, if you care about what you are writing, there is more of a chance that someone else will care.
