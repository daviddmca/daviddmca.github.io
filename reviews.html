Reviews

I love fiction, regardless of medium, and as I got more into the review and analysis aspect of it, and I watched videos and articles of people reviewing the stories that I love. I tend to not like talking about things I dislike, so as you will see, most of these reviews will be positive reviews, especially becuase the stories I want to talk about most, are the ones that I have the most fondess for.

The hope for these reviews, is to use them as a base, for what would be a more in-depth review of these stories, done in the form of either an essay or a video.
